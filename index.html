About me

Where UX thinking meets people strategy

I started my career as a UX designer — and that way of thinking never left me. Understanding people deeply, mapping their journeys, and iterating based on real feedback doesn't only work for products. It works for teams, cultures, and workplaces too.

After a decade designing digital experiences at Sofatutor, Opinary, and Onefootball, I transitioned fully into the People & Culture space. My work sits at the intersection of strategy, empathy, and facilitation.

Based in Rome, fluent in international remote-first environments, currently pursuing a Master in Event Management to expand how I design meaningful collective experiences.

My methodology

How I design experiences

01

Analyse

Every experience starts with a deep understanding of people, context, and objectives. I design formats that respect energy levels, introverted and extroverted personalities, and human rhythms.

02

Design

Nothing is left to chance: flows, timing, spaces, emotions. I architect every detail to create a coherent, purposeful experience that serves both the individual and the collective.

03

Experience & Remember

I create experiences and moments that last beyond photos. People may forget what you organized — but they always remember how you made them feel.
